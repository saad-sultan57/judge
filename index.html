Judges Panel

The World’s Most Experienced ,Independent And Trusted Judges

Eleftheria Germanaki

Panel Leader

Eleftheria Germanaki Agronomist, Panel Supervisor and Experienced taster. Mrs. Eleftheria Germanaki is an Agronomist with 30 years’ experience in modern cultivation techniques, agricultural sector development and olive oil quality evaluation. Currently she is the Director and Panel Supervisor in the Sensory Evaluation Laboratory of Crete ACR, located in Rethimnon/Crete. The Laboratory is certified under the National System of Accreditation Organoleptic Assessment (ESYD) and recognised by IOC.

Furthermore, she is a consultant and trainer for the virgin olive oil quality assurance. Mrs Germanaki is organizing seminars and training courses on olive oil quality, extraction and organoleptic characteristics within Greece and abroad. In addition, she is writing related articles in digital and printed media. Eleftheria is participating as experienced and certified judge, in a variety of International Olive Oil Competition since 2014, in USA, Italy, Israel, Japan, France and Greece.

She works closely with the scientific institutions of the island and the local authorities towards the improvement of the olive oil sector. Since 2015, in collaboration with the Prefecture of Crete, she is organizing the Cretan olive oil competition (COOC), in the facilities of her laboratory.

Claudio Vignoli - Italy

Claudio Vignoli is an Italian Oleologist and Master Miller with over 25 years of experience in the olive oil industry. From 1996 until the end of 2007, he worked as an Export Sales Manager for the world's leading manufacturer of olive oil mills. In 2007, he founded his own company, Claudio Vignoli Group (www.claudiovignoli.com), providing technical and marketing consultancy to olive oil producers around the world. Claudio Vignoli also provides his expertise in the olive oil industry to two multinational consulting firms: GLG, Gerson Lehrman Group and GUIDEPOINT GLOBAL LLC. In 2015, he founded a bottling company in the U.S. called Miami Olive Oil & Beyond LLC (www.miaoliveoil.com), which imports bulk EVOO from the Mediterranean and bottles in Miami, FL, for the retail and food service industry. With this company, he produces the private label of several major U.S. distribution companies, cruise lines, hotel and restaurant chains. His mission is to educate buyers, chefs, and other industry professionals to understand, appreciate, and select high-quality olive oils through in-store demos, tasting classes, workshops, etc. He is also an independent advisor for several brands in Australia, California, Uruguay, Spain, and Italy. In 2022, he founded a brand that bears his name, Vignoli Food, which produces premium quality EVOOs and Balsamic Vinegar of Modena (www.vignolifood.com). He has been a member of O.L.E.A. (Association of Olive Oil Experts and Tasters) since 2017. He is often involved as a keynote speaker, jury member in international olive oil competitions, and presenter at industry events in Australia, Argentina, Uruguay, and Peru. He is also a regular member of the jury at CIOOC, Canada International Olive Oil Competition.

Dr. Mirella Žanetić - Croatia

Dr. Mirella Žanetić, works as a researcher at the Institute for Adriatic Cultures in Split, Croatia, in the laboratory for quality control of VOO. She is panel leader of the Institute’s VOO professional panel group for sensory analyses of virgin olive oil. Dr. Zanetic has more than 20 years’ experience in sensory analysis of VOO and regularly participates as a panel member for national and international competitions. She is a panel member of the most prestigious competition in olive oil quality – NYIOOC organized by Olive Oil Times, for 4 years. She participated as expert judge in international panel group for the olive oil competition SOL d’ORO, in Verona, Italy, EVO IOOC 2021. in Palmi, Italy. Her scientific interest is mainly focused on the quality and typicality of VOOs of native varieties, with particular attention to bioactive compounds (antioxidants) responsible for health properties of olive oils and to the volatile compounds that carry the aroma and sensory attributes of VOOs. Dr. Zanetic also works at the University of Split, as assistant professor at the Faculty of Mediterranean agriculture. She has conducted numerous seminars/ workshops on quality improvement and guided tasting of VOO for olive growers and consumers, as well as educational interactive seminars and workshops for teachers and students. Dr. Zanetic has published different scientific articles in reputable international scientific journals, with significant impact factor.

Karpadakis Emmanouil - Greece

Born οn the island of Crete in Greece, he has studied and worked initially as an engineer and later in the field of international relations.

For more than 16 years he has been working in the olive oil sector as a professional in the olive oil production, innovation, communication, exports and sensory evaluation with long-time experience in judging for international olive oil competitions.

Trained by IOC instructors, today he is a member of the certified panel of tasters in the olive oil sensory evaluation lab of Crete and in addition, he is the Vice President of the Cretan Exporters Association.

Being expert in his field with recognized knowledge, he has present olive oil related subjects in International fairs and events as invited speaker (IOC summit, FHC summit, HORECA forum, SIMEI etc, ) Emmanouil speaks Greek, English and Italian.

Javier Sánchez-Pedrós - Spain

Javier Sánchez-Pedrós holds a university degree in geography and history, but during the last 20 years he has been working in the olive oil bussiness from the traditional method of hidraulic presses to the most advanced mills. He is the master miller, production and quality director of the award winning company Aceites Impelte del Bajo Martín, independent advisor for several award winning companies and brands and teaches in training courses organized in the North of Spain. Since 2006 Sánchez-Pedrós is member of the “Panel de Catadores de AOV de Aragón” recognized by the International Olive Oil Council and the Spanish Ministry for Agriculture, Fisheries and Food and located in Alcañiz (Aragón). Since 2010 he is a judge for several national and international extra virgin olive oil competitions (Mejor Aceite del Bajo Aragón, TerraOlivo, EVO-IOOC Italy, Canada IOOC, Anatolian IOOC, Olivonomy Paris Awards). In 2019 he was awarded with the prize “Maestro de Almazara” (Master Miller) at Oleomaq International Fair (International Olive OIl Mill Machinery, Equipment & Packing Show).

Dr. Sonda Laroussi

Dr. Sonda Laroussi is a renowned international olive oil expert, specializing in sensory analysis training for table olives and virgin olive oil. With a passion for elevating the standards of the industry, she has guided and supported numerous sensory analysis laboratories from inception to International Olive Council (IOC) recognition and ISO 17025 accreditation.

As the visionary founder and panel leader of “Olea Conseils,” Tunisia’s first private sensory panel, Dr. Laroussi has successfully transformed it into a premier technical center dedicated to comprehensive olive oil analysis, studies, and training.

Dr. Laroussi’s commitment to innovation is further exemplified by her role as co-founder and CEO of ‘220’, a pioneering agritech startup that bridges the gap between olive oil traders and producers, aiming to enhance olive oil quality and improve farmers’ livelihoods. Driven by her deep-rooted passion for the olive oil sector, she actively educates producers, consumers, and children alike on olive oil culture, quality, and tasting techniques, ensuring a brighter future for this cherished industry.

We are thrilled to have Dr. Laroussi as our expert for the upcoming European International Olive Oil Competition (EIOOC) in Geneva 2023. Her wealth of knowledge and experience will undoubtedly contribute to the success of the event, elevating its prestige and impact within the olive oil community

Philippe Bruyninckx - Belgium

Philippe Bruyninckx is a Belgian, lives 40 km east of Brussels. From my childhood I was raised between the perfumes my mother sold. So my nose was very well trained from very young, an advantage I am still happy with. For many years I worked in product engineering but switched to independent worker in 2012. I have a degree in Biochemistry and have a company in telemarketing for scientifical (laboratory) equipment. The love for EVOO started 30 years ago in Crete. I always want to know more about it, but professional reasons didn’t allow me to do that. To know more and better understand this great product I went to ONAOO in 2015 and became technical olive oil taster. In 2018 I succeeded and got the degree of professional olive oil taster at ONAOO. Up to now, I am the only one in Belgium. EVOO became a passion and the degree from ONAOO was the extra boost to spread the knowledge. I import extra virgin olive oils and olives from the Mediterranean. I give workshops and love to bring the knowledge about EVOO to the public. I am also a member and vice panel leader of the olive oil institute of the Netherlands. This gives me the opportunity for constantly tasting and judging different samples. The samples are the complete range in quality EVOO and unfortunately non EVOO.
